About

For the past six years, I've been building mobile apps that people use every day.

Based in Manila, I started my career as an Android Developer in 2019 and have since worked on products across entertainment, health, pet care, fintech, and media industries. Along the way, I've grown from an individual contributor into a Team Leader, delivering applications from concept to production.

My experience spans the entire mobile development lifecycle—from building Android and Flutter applications from a blank repository to taking over unfinished codebases and successfully bringing them to production. I've worked extensively with Firebase, Firestore, REST APIs, and managed releases through the Google Play Console from initial launch to ongoing updates.

I enjoy solving complex technical challenges, whether it's architecting new features, improving app performance, or stabilizing legacy systems. My focus is always on building reliable, scalable, and maintainable applications that continue to perform long after launch.

I believe great software isn't just about shipping features—it's about creating products that remain fast, stable, and easy for the next developer to understand, maintain, and improve.
